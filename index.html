Our Solutions

High-Performance Sludge Management

Specifically engineered for commercial hog barns and liquid agricultural setups. When dropped directly into deep storage pits, our organic Pit Pucks actively trigger focused microbial digestion cascades.

This process systematically breaks down heavy bottom solids, liquefies thick crusting blockages, drastically reduces ambient barn ammonia and odor hazards, and keeps volatile elements bound securely in the liquid pool to boost final manure fertilizer field application value.

Ecological Aquatic Bio-Remediation

Reclaim natural, clean balance in your farm ponds, commercial waterways, or recreational lakes without resorting to harsh synthetic copper sulfate treatments.

Deployed straight into target waters as Pond Pucks, these all-organic pucks sink to target the source of the problem. They continually release specific biological enzymes that naturally digest built-up bottom organic muck and isolate excess nutrient chains, directly cutting off the food sources that generate toxic summer algae blooms.

Rebuilding Native Biological Foundations

True field longevity starts from the subsoil up. Our core treatment focus centers heavily on reducing structural reliance on traditional chemical additives by activating inherent soil biology.

By maximizing organic nutrient absorption directly within organic livestock applications and promoting biological solutions, we aid growers in optimizing subterranean soil roots, driving crop residue breakdown speeds, and maximizing field moisture preservation capacity safely.
